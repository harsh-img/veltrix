Quick FAQs

Answers to common questions about scope, timelines, and support.

How long does a website or web app take? ⌄

Most websites take 1–3 weeks. Web apps depend on features, but we usually ship an MVP in 3–6 weeks with clear milestones.

Do you provide hosting, domains, and maintenance? ⌄

Yes. We can manage hosting + deployments, performance, security updates, and ongoing improvements (or hand over clean documentation to your team).

Can you integrate WhatsApp, payments, or CRMs? ⌄

Absolutely. We regularly connect websites/apps with WhatsApp flows, payment gateways, analytics, email/CRM tools, and custom APIs.
