Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Vernaxify Tech (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our website or contact us.

Information we collect

  • Contact information you submit (name, phone/WhatsApp, email)
  • Message content you send through forms or email
  • Basic technical data (browser/device) may be collected by your email provider or hosting provider

How we use your information

  • To respond to your enquiry and provide quotations

Sharing of data

We do not sell your personal data. We may share necessary information with tools used for communication and delivery (for example, email, hosting, project management) only as needed to provide services.

Data security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no system is 100% secure; please avoid sending passwords or sensitive personal information via email.

Retention

We retain information only as long as needed for communication, service delivery, and legal/accounting requirements.

Contact
